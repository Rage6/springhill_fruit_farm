OUR MISSION
From our orchard to your home, our goal is to provide you with a quality product, a friendly service, and a customer satisfaction guarentee!
The farm was purchased by Kenneth and MaryJane Burrer in the spring of 1968, and it was named "Springhill Fruit Farm" based on the natural springs on the property. Their youngest son, Jeff Burrer, started leasing the farm in 1998. He and his wife, Laura, took official ownership of Springhill Fruit Farm on December 16th, 2003.
ALL OF JEFF AND LAURA'S FAMILY MEMBERS ARE A BLESSED PART OF THEIR TEAM. THEY INCLUDE THEIR SON (BENJAMIN) WITH HIS WIFE (KEELY) AND THEIR DAUGHTER (ISLA), THEIR DAUGHTER (MANDY MILLER) WITH HER HUSBAND (DANIEL) AND THEIR CHILDREN (PARKER, LUKE, AND ELLEE), AND YOUNGEST DAUGHTER (MEGAN) WITH HER FIANCEE (KEVIN SHERER).
SPRINGHILL FRUIT FARM STARTED WITH AROUND 1000 TREES AND, AS OF 2018, HAS EXPANDED TO OVER 5000 TREES! IT INCLUDES OVER 30 VARIETIES OF APPLES, 4 VARIETIES OF PEARS, 6 VARIETIES OF PEACHES, 3 VARIETIES OF PLUMS AND 2 VARIETIES OF NECTARINES. ADDITIONALLY, IT GROWS PUMPKINS AND SQUASH, AND PROCESSES CIDER.
